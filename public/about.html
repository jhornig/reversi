About Reversi

Reversi is a strategy board game for two players, played on an 8Ã—8 uncheckered board.

Game play

The black player goes first by capturing a white piece.

Pieces are captured by sandwiching them horizontally, vertically, or diagonally between two of your own pieces.



The captured white piece then becomes a black piece.

Players take turns, each time capturing at least one of their opponent's pieces.

Object

The object of the game is to have captured more pieces than your opponent when the game is over.

The game is over when neither player has a move.
